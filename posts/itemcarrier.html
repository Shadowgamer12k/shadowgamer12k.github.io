ABOUT

Minecraft Items Carrier Add-on This addon will help you move items from one chest to another. It also works with other addons and is simple to use

Built specifically for Minecraft Bedrock Edition 1.21 and above, this add-on integrates seamlessly with your gameplay, providing an intuitive interface for creating, managing, and navigating between important locations in your world.

FEATURES :

  • 🎉 move items from one chest to another.
  • 🎉 Simpl and easy to use.
  • 🎉 Works With other addons.

HOW TO USE

Just hold the item in your hand, sneak, and click on the first chest or container from which you want to take the items. Then you go to the other empty chest or container in which you want to put the items that you took from the first chest. Then click on it too, and a menu will appear for you containing a button with the confirmation of the transfer. Press the button. The items will be transferred automatically :):

-> Main Form :

To move items from betwen chests, you need to craft the Items Carrier item first

INSTALLATION GUIDE

  1. Download both the Behavior Pack and Resource Pack
  2. Open Minecraft and navigate to Settings > Global Resources
  3. Click on "Import" and select the downloaded .mcpack files
  4. Create a new world or edit an existing one
  5. Under world settings, enable both the Item Carrier Behavior Pack and Resource Pack
  6. Ensure "Require Trusted Packages" is disabled if you encounter any issues
