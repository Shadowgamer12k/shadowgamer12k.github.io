Bioscape V2 is a transformative addon for Minecraft Bedrock Edition that breathes new life into your world. It is designed to completely overhaul the natural environment, making exploration feel fresh, immersive, and visually stunning without straying too far from the vanilla aesthetic.

This expansion introduces a vast array of new procedural generation features. From towering, realistic trees to dense, lush ground foliage, every biome receives a significant upgrade. Whether you are trekking through thick forests or discovering new meadow variations, Bioscape V2 ensures that nature looks more organic and alive than ever before.

üéÆ HOW TO USE

1. WORLD CREATION

Create a new world in Minecraft Bedrock. This addon relies heavily on custom generation, so it works best on fresh seeds rather than old worlds.

2. ENABLE EXPERIMENTS

You MUST enable "Holiday Creator Features" and "Custom Biomes" (if available) in the Experiments tab. Without these, the new terrain will not generate.

3. EXPLORE

Once loaded, simply travel! The new biomes will appear naturally as you explore the Overworld.

WILL THIS LAG MY DEVICE?
Bioscape V2 adds a lot of foliage. While optimized, it may cause frame drops on older mobile devices or the Nintendo Switch.
IS IT COMPATIBLE WITH OTHER ADDONS?
It edits world generation, so it may conflict with other addons that also add custom biomes or change terrain generation.
