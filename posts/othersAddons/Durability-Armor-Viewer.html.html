üß© ABOUT

The Durability Armor Viewer addon is a must-have utility for any PvP or Survival player in Minecraft Bedrock. It removes the guesswork from combat by displaying a clean, real-time HUD (Heads-Up Display) of your armor's status directly on your screen.

No more opening your inventory mid-fight to check if your chestplate is about to break. This lightweight addon shows durability percentages and tool life, ensuring you know exactly when to swap gear or retreat to repair. It is designed to be unobtrusive, blending perfectly with the vanilla UI.

‚ú® KEY FEATURES

ARMOR STATUS HUD

Displays icons for your Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, and Boots directly on the main screen.

DURABILITY COUNTERS

Shows exact durability or damage bars, letting you know how many hits your gear has left.

TOOL TRACKING

Also tracks the durability of the item currently held in your main hand (Swords, Pickaxes, etc.).

CLEAN UI

Minimalist design that doesn't clutter your screen, perfect for competitive PvP scenarios.

üéÆ HOW TO USE

1. INSTALLATION

Download the .mcpack file and import it into Minecraft. Apply it to your Global Resources or specific world Resource Packs.

2. ACTIVATION

Ensure the pack is at the top of your resource pack list. No experimental toggles are usually required for basic UI changes, but check the pack details.

Simply equip armor or hold a tool. The HUD will automatically appear, typically in the bottom-left or bottom-right corner of your screen.
