üéÆ HOW TO USE

1. CRAFTING THE BACKPACK

To begin, you will need to craft the backpack using Leather and a Chest. Combine them in the crafting table to receive the backpack item.

2. EQUIPPING & OPENING

Once crafted, hold the backpack in your hand. Sneak (Crouch) and Interact (Right-Click/Tap) to equip it to your back. To open the inventory, sneak and jump or use the custom item provided.
