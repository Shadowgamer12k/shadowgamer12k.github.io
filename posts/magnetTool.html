OVERVIEW

Stop running around to pick up drops. Magnet Tech Ultimate introduces a sophisticated, upgradeable item attraction system to Minecraft Bedrock.

Powered by your own XP, this isn't just a simple "teleport items" script. It simulates magnetic pull physics, features a realistic Thermodynamic Heat System (overheating mechanics), and includes a built-in In-Game Workshop UI to upgrade your tech.

Whether you are mining, farming, or fighting, the magnet pulls resources directly to you. Unlock "Auto-Loot" to skip the ground entirely and inject items straight into your inventory.

üõ†Ô∏è UPGRADE WORKSHOP

Sneak + Right Click with the Magnet to open the Workshop Menu.

Spend your XP Levels to permanently upgrade your stats. The addon saves your progress using `DynamicProperties`, so your upgrades persist across sessions.

  • Range: Increase radius (Max Lvl 5).
  • Speed: Pull items faster (Max Lvl 3).
  • Cooling: Reduce cooldowns (Max Lvl 3).

üå°Ô∏è THERMODYNAMICS

The magnet generates heat while active. This is visualized via the item's durability bar.

If you run it too long, it will Overheat and lock down with a "COOLING DOWN" status. You must wait for the heat to dissipate before using it again.

Tip: Upgrade your "Cooling" stat in the workshop to vent heat faster!

üéí AUTO-LOOT & SIPHON

Unlock advanced modules in the shop:

XP Siphon:
Enables the magnet to attract XP Orbs, not just items. Perfect for mob farms.

Auto-Loot:
Bypasses physics entirely. Items within range are instantly teleported into your inventory slots. No more lag from floating entities.
